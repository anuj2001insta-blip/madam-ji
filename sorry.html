A Letter of Apology

Written with a heavy heart & trembling hands

KIET, 27 March 2026

My dearest Madam ji,

I sit here tonight, pen in hand (naam ka pen hai heheheh), staring at a blank page — knowing that no words I write will ever be quite enough. And yet, I must try. Because you deserve more than silence, more than excuses, and infinitely more than the hurt I have caused you.

You never asked for anything complicated from me. You only ever asked to feel seen, to feel safe, to feel like you matter. And you do — more than anyone, more than everything. Yet in the moments that mattered most, I made you feel otherwise. That is something I will not forgive myself for easily, nor should I.

⁂

If I could go back, main sab se phle vo sab thik karta jo main galat kiya hu I would have said "I'm sorry"

So here it is, written in ink so it cannot be taken back: I am sorry, with every part of me. I am sorry for the tum ko hurt kiyaaaa. I am sorry for the carelessness. I am sorry for the times you reached for me aur main kise aur se baat krta rha and I was not fully there. You are not too much. You have never been too much. I was simply not enough — and I am working to change that.
